High Speed Rail in the United States of America

Current High Speed Rail Projects Underway

Although the United States only has one currently active High Speed Rail Corridor by definition, there are various projects in different stages of development across the U.S.. These projects vary in scope and size but will have notable impacts on their respective regions once they have been completed.
